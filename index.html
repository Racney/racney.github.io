Financial News

S&P 500 and Dow Reach Record Highs: Both the S&P 500 and Dow Jones hit record levels, supported by a surge in tech stocks like Nvidia. The S&P 500 is up about 21% so far this year, marking one of its best first-three-quarter performances of the 21st century. While there are some concerns about interest rates and a possible recession, analysts remain optimistic about the outlook for the fourth quarter​ ​

Uber Near Record Highs: Uber shares have also been trading near record highs, with the stock recently reaching $87. Despite a slight dip, investors remain bullish, especially after Tesla's robotaxi unveiling, which posed no immediate threat to Uber's core business​

Bitcoin Surge: Bitcoin prices have risen close to $66,000, lifting cryptocurrency-related stocks such as Coinbase and Marathon Digital. The broader market is benefiting from this rally, with tech-heavy indices like the Nasdaq climbing along with crypto stocks​
